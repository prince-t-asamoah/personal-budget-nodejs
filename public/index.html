Simple Process

Get Started in Minutes

Our intuitive system makes envelope budgeting easy, even if you've never tried it before.

1

Create Your Envelopes

Start by creating envelopes for your spending categories. Add as many as you need - groceries, rent, utilities, entertainment, savings, and more. Name them whatever makes sense for your life.

2

Allocate Your Income

When you get paid, distribute the money across your envelopes. You can do this manually or use our smart distribution feature to split funds automatically. Every cedi gets a purpose.

3

Track Your Spending

As you spend throughout the month, record your expenses in the appropriate envelopes. Watch your balances update in real-time and see exactly how much you have left for each category.
